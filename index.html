How it works

Learn the practice

Attend or book a program and learn the practice. Once you complete the program, you will be able to do the practice on your own. Post-program support is also available.

Practice at home

At home, you can do the practice by yourself and incorporate it into your daily life. This gives you the convenience of practicing where and when you want.

Attend a review session

After some time, if you wish to deepen your practice or receive corrections, you can attend a review session.

After you have learnt the practice, we can have guided sessions for your practice to support your practice.

About the Teacher

Hi, my name is Sankalp. Since childhood, I naturally possessed a deep longing to experience higher dimensions of energy. In my early twenties, I tried various tools to explore this—learning yoga through books and videos—but the moment I stumbled upon the practices offered by Isha, I knew there was no looking back or searching elsewhere.

In March 2018, while in my 3rd year at Trinity College Dublin, I attended the Inner Engineering program in Dublin and had a profoundly transformative experience. This inspired me to learn furthur Isha practices and I learnt Classical Hatha Yoga from a local teacher.

I completed the rigorous Teacher Training Program at the Isha Yoga Center in India and became a Classical Hatha Yoga teacher, officially certified by Sadhguru Gurukulam, in December 2025.

After six years of dedicated daily practice, I am deeply moved to contribute to Sadhguru’s vision of global well-being by offering these transformative tools to the world.
